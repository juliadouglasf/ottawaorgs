About

Maybe you want to join a club, start volunteering, or donate to a local organisation. It can be difficult to find local organisations based on a topic of interest.

  • Google Maps prioritises commercial businesses and completely excludes organisations without permanent physical locations.
  • Search engines tend to spotlight larger organisations, and most people won't go through multiple pages of results to find a smaller organisation that may be exactly what they're looking for.
  • Other attempts have been made to create similar topic-based directories, but they often lack functionality and/or are out-of-date.

Ottawa Orgs aims to make it easier to find a relevant organisation.

  • Alphabetised results
  • Search functionality (in development)
  • Topic-specific filters (in development)
  • Map of results (in development)

Ottawa Orgs was created as a personal project by Julia Douglas Freitas (juliadouglasf on GitHub). Visit the Contribute page to help improve Ottawa Orgs!
